Short Bio

​I am currently a second year (2022fall -) bachelor-straight-to-Ph.D. student in the School of Electronic Information and Communications (EIC), Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), supervised by Dr. Zengqiang Yan and Prof. Li Yu. Before that, I received the B. Eng. degree also from EIC, HUST in 2022.

My research interests lie in developing robust AI systems under real-world constraints (distributed datasets, imperfect labels, class imbalance, etc.).
